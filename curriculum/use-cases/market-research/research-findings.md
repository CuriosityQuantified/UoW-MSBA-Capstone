## PwC Research

### 2026 AI Business Predictions
**Source:** PwC, January 2026
**Summary:** Only a few companies are realizing extraordinary value from AI today—achieving surging top-line growth and significant valuation premiums. The report identifies that success is becoming visible, with clear patterns emerging for using AI to build leading-edge operating models. Agentic AI is highlighted as playing an increasingly important role, with the disciplined march to value beginning through enterprise-wide strategy centered on top-down programs. AI studios (centralized hubs bringing together reusable tech components, frameworks, and skilled people) are recommended to link business goals to AI capabilities.
**Link:** https://www.pwc.com/us/en/tech-effect/ai-analytics/ai-predictions.html

### AI Agents for Finance: Transforming the Operating Model
**Source:** PwC, February 2026
**Summary:** Finance functions are at a tipping point where traditional cost efficiencies are peaking while demand for actionable insights accelerates. The report details how AI agents can deliver up to 90% time savings in key processes, redirect up to 60% of team time to insight work, and improve forecasting accuracy and speed by up to 40%. With 79% of executives reporting AI agent adoption but only 34% using them in finance, there's significant opportunity for transformation in areas like procure-to-pay, where AI-driven invoice extraction can slash cycle times by up to 80%.
**Link:** https://www.pwc.com/us/en/tech-effect/ai-analytics/ai-agents-for-finance.html

### 5 Ways CHROs Can Lead with AI Agents
**Source:** PwC, March 2026
**Summary:** AI agents are shifting HR from defense to offense, taking on routine tasks so teams can focus on driving business impact. The report finds that agents can reduce human effort by 40-50% across HR functions, with talent sourcing savings reaching 70% for hiring managers. Despite 79% of executives adopting AI agents, only 40% use them in HR. The analysis reveals that over 88% of administrative HR workflows can be agent-enabled, with the new operating model enabling HR to transition from processing to strategic advisory work.
**Link:** https://www.pwc.com/us/en/tech-effect/ai-analytics/agentic-ai-in-hr.html

### AI Agents for IT: A New Operating Model
**Source:** PwC, March 2026
**Summary:** AI agents are reshaping the IT operating model by taking on routine tasks to multiply capacity and free specialists for strategy and innovation. With 53% of US businesses deploying AI agents already using them in IT and cybersecurity, early adopters are seeing agents take on up to 50% of IT teams' daily tasks. The report emphasizes that success requires capturing pre-AI metrics (cycle times, service levels, operating expenses) to track value delivery across four dimensions: reduced costs, enhanced performance, rebalanced workload, and transformed workforce model.
**Link:** https://www.pwc.com/us/en/tech-effect/ai-analytics/agentic-ai-in-it.html

## Forrester Research

### Take Control of Your AI Voyage — Your Customers Deserve It
**Source:** Forrester, February 2026
**Summary:** Based on a survey of 1,500 AI decision-makers, this report identifies four critical traits that set high-adopting firms apart: customer-led approaches (focusing on visible customer value), CEO-driven strategy (top-down approach with leadership alignment), data and platform readiness (foundational investments in governance and infrastructure), and high AIQ (prioritizing talent with high AI aptitude). The research finds that despite experimentation, few organizations have translated early AI innovation into meaningful business impact, with productivity gains often incremental and customer experience at an all-time low in North America.
**Link:** https://www.forrester.com/blogs/take-control-of-your-ai-voyage/

### Agentish Vs. Agentic in Go-To-Market: Choose Control Over Autonomy
**Source:** Forrester, March 2026
**Summary:** This analysis distinguishes between "agentish" (AI assists within defined workflows with human accountability) and fully agentic AI (independent goal-setting and end-to-end execution) in revenue-focused systems. The report argues that for revenue systems, value is built in sequence—accuracy, governance, safety, then autonomy—and platforms inverting this order risk eroding trust and amplifying errors. Most current revtech platforms operate in an "agentish" state by design, with fully agentic AI remaining aspirational in revenue contexts due to accountability concerns, audit trail requirements, and trust preservation needs.
**Link:** https://www.forrester.com/blogs/agentish-vs-agentic-in-gtm-choose-control-over-autonomy/

### RSAC 2026: An AI Coming-Of-Age Story Without The Romance
**Source:** Forrester, April 2026
**Summary:** Coverage of the 2026 RSA Conference (43,500+ attendees) reveals key AI security trends: Anthropic emerged as the frontier lab most visibly active in cybersecurity, traditional vendors are finally getting serious about identity security, and the "agentic SOC" (security operations center) is ascendant. The report notes that 50% of organizations are piloting agentic AI while 24% have it in production, yet security teams are catching up after the fact. Forrester recommends prioritizing utility, trust, and cost when evaluating generative AI capabilities in security tools.
**Link:** https://www.forrester.com/blogs/rsac-2026-an-ai-coming-of-age-story-without-the-romance/

### Agentic Commerce: Lessons from Social Commerce
**Source:** Forrester, March 2026
**Summary:** Drawing parallels between social commerce's evolution and the emerging agentic commerce era, this report identifies four key lessons: show consumers tangible value to change habits, get customer experience right before launch, ensure the right channel for the right customer, and foster trust in the channel. The analysis notes that 62% of US online adults don't trust social media companies to protect user information—a barrier that extends to payment info and affects agentic commerce adoption. The report recommends that brands apply drop-shipping fundamentals and maintain quality product data feeds for new commerce channels.
**Link:** https://www.forrester.com/blogs/agentic-commerce-era-lessons-from-social-commerce-and-digital-ad-channels/

### How CommBank Blends Customer, Digital, and AI Leadership
**Source:** Forrester, February 2026
**Summary:** This case study examines Commonwealth Bank of Australia's integrated executive role combining customer, digital, and AI leadership. With 99% of retail customer interactions already digital, the bank is making interactions more conversational and intuitive through virtual agents. The report highlights how the convergence of data, digital, and AI is being managed with strong governance from the start, emphasizing trust as paramount in banking. The bank also uses agentic AI internally for onboarding, compliance, and customer support, demonstrating how traditional financial institutions are structuring for AI transformation.
**Link:** https://www.forrester.com/blogs/how-commbank-blends-customer-digital-and-ai-leadership/
